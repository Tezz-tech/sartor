ABOUT US

We're A Premium Management Consulting Firm

At Sartor, we focus on entrepreneurs, startups and MSMEs as these are the predominant drivers of the African economy, many of whom may lack access to funding or possess the skills necessary to launch, grow and lead their startups into successful businesses.

We offer free online lectures, webinars, tools, links, and resources to help entrepreneurs launch, grow and lead their businesses into successful businesses.

We also offer one-on-one virtual or in-person consulting services, retainers and mentorship programs to help entrepreneurs overcome challenges in running a startup.

From business registration in Nigeria to business planning, product, service development, branding, marketing and developing an effective work culture we’ve got you covered.

We understand that the business landscape is constantly evolving that’s why we study changes and advancements in technologies, policies and macroeconomy thereby helping us navigate the business landscape to our objectives.

We identify your uniqueness to help us deliver tailored solutions to your needs.

We don’t just give business advisory, we walk the walk with you.
